Welcome to Velammal Engineering College (VEC)

Established in 1995, Velammal Engineering College (VEC) is a premier institution affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE. It is located in Surapet, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The college is a part of the Velammal Educational Trust, which is known for delivering quality education across Tamil Nadu.

Our Vision

To strive for excellence in technical education by producing competent professionals with strong ethical values and leadership qualities.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality education with a strong emphasis on practical knowledge.
  • Encourage innovation, research, and entrepreneurship.
  • Build a responsible and skilled workforce for national and global development.

Key Highlights

  • NBA Accredited Programs
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • State-of-the-art laboratories and infrastructure
  • More than 500+ campus placements annually
  • Strong alumni network across top companies worldwide

Departments

VEC offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs through departments such as:

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Campus Life

The VEC campus provides an environment that promotes academic growth, physical well-being, and personal development. With smart classrooms, digital libraries, well-equipped labs, hostels, sports facilities, and various student clubs, the campus offers a holistic education experience.
